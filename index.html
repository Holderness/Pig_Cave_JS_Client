About

Pig Cave is a website paying homage to dreaming, most often experienced in sleep, but also in the quiet desperation of boredom. Its goal is to simulate connections between stories and thoughts that otherwise would go unearthed.

Pig Cave has three stages: Story, Thought, and Dream. First, a Story. Pig Cave gives you a random six-word-story (e.g. "For sale: baby shoes, never worn."). From here your story enters a finely tuned word-matching algorithm called the Prism of Ontological Recumbent Kinetic Energy and Rumplestiltkin Syzygy, or PORKERS. PORKERS spits out a similarly themed Thought and, lastly, slaps the Story and Thought together to conjure a Dream. And voila! Your own dream machine. Makes Sense? No? Kind of? Does this brief synopsis arouse a sense of dread akin to the infinite weight of a rubix cube pressing into your chest that only a 20ft mathematician and an octopus in a bucket can solve? Great! That's the dreaming spirit. Welcome to Pig Cave.

* Pig Cave uses data from three APIs stored on a separate server. The original data was collected from Reddit.com and DreamBank.net.
