About

Pig Cave is a story-telling website paying homage to dreams, most often experienced in sleep, but also in the quiet desperation of boredom. Ever have a past experience enter your dreams in a strange and surpising way? That is the process Pig Cave attempts to mimic.

The Process

First, a random six-word story. From there your Story enters a finely tuned word-matching algorithm called the Prism of Ontological Recumbent Kinetic Energy and Rumplestiltkin Syzygy, or PORKERS. PORKERS spits out an oddly related Thought, and then digests the material into full-blown, no-holes-bard Dream. And voila! Your own dream machine. Makes sense? No? Kind of? Does this brief synopsis arouse dread akin to the infinite weight of a rubix cube pressing into your chest that only a 20ft mathematician and an octopus in a bucket can solve? Great!
